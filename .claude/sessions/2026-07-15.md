## Session summary

Designed the full LrC workflow from scratch. Settled on T7 as the portable catalogue-and-photos drive, used on both iMac and MBP by plugging in.

**Folder structure on T7 (finalised):**
- `photos/YYYY/MM/` — RAWs + round-trip TIFFs
- `exports/YYYY/` — curated finals for web/portfolio
- `catalogue/` — LrC catalogue (created this session)
- `inbox/` and `working/` — removed as unnecessary

**Workflow decisions:**
- Import direct from card to `photos/`, no inbox staging
- Cull with `X` flags in LrC, delete from disk in one pass
- Metadata in catalogue only; XMP sidecars disabled (confirmed in Catalog Settings)
- Catalogue backups enabled on both machines, pointing to NAS

**Rsync extended:** `t7-backup.sh` now syncs `exports/` → `/Volumes/photos/photography-exports/`. Committed and pushed to Gitea; pulled on MBP.
